## 🪐 The Core Philosophy: Digital Gravity
In Skydrops, data has mass. As a "Drop" resonates with the community, its gravitational pull increases, allowing it to transcend the noise of the atmosphere.

### 🏗️ Atmospheric Layers

* **The Zenith (The Visual Sanctuary):** The apex of the Skydrops experience. Once a transmission gains sufficient gravity, it resolves into full-fidelity focus. This is a multimedia sanctuary where every Drop—whether audio, text, high-res imagery, or 4K video—is rendered through the weightless **Ether UI**. It is a place for deep consumption and crystalline clarity.
    
* **The Meadow (The Audible Foundation):** The grounded, organic discovery layer. The Meadow is intentionally audible, driven by the resonance of voice and sound. It is where you "hear" the heartbeat of the network before the visuals come into focus. It remains the foundation of all digital life in Skydrops.

* **The Bedrock:** The raw, decentralized storage layer (SQLite + ATProto Repo) where your data is cryptographically signed and sovereign. Seen through the retro-future **8-Bit Mode**.

## 🎨 Visual Frequencies
1.  **Ether UI (Zenith Level):** Smooth glassmorphism and sharp clarity for multimedia consumption.
2.  **Mist UI (Meadow Level):** Abstract, pulsing visuals that respond to audio frequency and resonance.
3.  **8-Bit Mode (Bedrock Level):** Dithered, retro-tech aesthetic for raw data management.
