# 🌌 Skydrops.app
**Where the Skies touch Grass.**

Skydrops is a sovereign, decentralized audio-to-video experience built on the **AT Protocol**. It is designed to move beyond the traditional "social media feed" by implementing a physics-based discovery model known as **Digital Gravity**.

## 🪐 The Core Philosophy: Digital Gravity
In Skydrops, content isn't forced by a hidden cloud algorithm. Instead, every "Drop" has **Mass**. As a Drop resonates with the community (through Likes and Echos), its gravitational pull increases, pulling it from the grounded **Meadow** into the high-altitude **Zenith**.

### 🏗️ Architectural Layers
* **The Meadow:** The organic, local-first discovery layer. This is where all transmissions begin.
* **The Zenith:** A high-fidelity, curated space for resonant content. It features a unique **Flare UI** aesthetic.
* **The Event Horizon:** The threshold where a Drop gains enough mass to be officially "pinned" to your personal Zenith.

## 🤖 Meet Helia: Your Sovereign AI
Helia is the "Gravity Lens" of Skydrops. Unlike traditional AI assistants, Helia:
* **Lives on your device:** Runs locally on your hardware (optimized for Pixel 9 NPU) using Gemini Nano.
* **Speaks your language:** Native AAVE support and a grounded, helpful personality.
* **Understands the Protocol:** Acts as a sovereign ATProto Labeler, organizing your Zenith based on community-signed labels and curation lists.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & AT Protocol Integration
* **Platform:** Android (Kotlin/Compose)
* **Identity:** DID-based, sovereign user handles (e.g., `v.skydrops.app`).
* **Database:** Local SQLite "Bedrock" for metadata + Cloudflare D1 for relaying.
* **Media Storage:** High-fidelity audio/video hosted on Cloudflare R2.
* **Lexicons:** Custom schemas for `Drops`, `Echos`, and `ZenithSort` registered in the Lexicon Garden.

## 🎨 Visual Modes
Skydrops supports two distinct "Frequencies":
1.  **Ether UI: ** Smooth glassmorphism, glowing nebulas, and fluid physics-based transitions.
2.  **8-Bit Mode:** A dithered, retro-future aesthetic for a nostalgic, low-power vibe.

## ☁️ Success-Tax Protection
Built to be efficient. By offloading indexing to the local device and using lazy-blob fetching from R2, Skydrops remains performant and cost-effective regardless of network scale.
